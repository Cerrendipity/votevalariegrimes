Meet Valarie

Who Valarie Is

Valarie Grimes is not running as a politician. She is stepping forward as a nurse, entrepreneur, ministry founder, and mother who has spent twelve years building trust in Savannah.

  • A registered nurse and wellness professional who has spent her career caring for people, not managing constituents.
  • A community builder: founder of a local ministry, owner of a Savannah small business, and a 12-year resident of the district.
  • A parent with two grandchildren in the public school system she is running to serve. She has a direct, personal stake in the outcome.
  • A collaborator who says plainly: "it's not about Republican and Democrat, it's about doing the right thing for the citizens."
  • A first-time candidate who is deliberate, prepared, and already trusted by local pastors, entrepreneurs, and parents before day one of the campaign.

"I will listen first, then lead, and I will not stop asking why every student in this county doesn't already have a general level of quality education."
